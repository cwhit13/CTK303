Yo, this is the top song for the day of August 25, 1997

Mo Money, Mo Problems

by the Notorious B.I.G. Featuring Puff Daddy and Mase

The song is Notorious B.I.G.'s second posthumous number one single, following "Hypnotize", making him the only artist in Hot 100 history to have two number one singles posthumously.
The song was nominated for the 1998 Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group.
