Charlotte,
The Little Vampire of the Prairie

Charlotte has forgotten what year she was born,

but she has seen the rise and fall of many kingdoms.

She came to America with the pilgrims. Charlotte was hoping to find a better life.

Now she wanders through the fields and prairies, praying upon hapless victims.

Beware, for her favorite victims are people who identify as a "Foodie, hiker, loves to travel"!
